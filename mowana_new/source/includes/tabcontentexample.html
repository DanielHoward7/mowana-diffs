About the Property

Pearl Valley is well established 5 star residential and leisure resort midway between Franschhoek and Paarl, roughly 25 minutes drive from Stellenbosch and 50 minutes from Cape Town. It boasts the Number 5 rated golf course of SA and has international recognition for this Jack Nicklaus course. The estate offers a variety of different property propositions depending on one’s needs and lifestyle.
